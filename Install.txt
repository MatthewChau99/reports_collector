For Unix users:

Installing xpdf:

1. Copy the xpdf directory to /usr/local/share

2. Copy the executables in bin64 (xpdf, pdftotext, etc.) to to /usr/local/bin.

3. Copy the man pages (*.1 and *.5) to /usr/local/man/man1 and
   /usr/local/man/man5.

4. Copy doc/sample-xpdfrc file to /usr/local/etc/xpdfrc.
